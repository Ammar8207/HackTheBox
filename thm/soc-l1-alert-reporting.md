# THM - SOC L1 Alert Reporting
**Platform:** TryHackMe

## What It Covered
How to properly document, report, and escalate 
high-risk SOC alerts.

## What I Learned
- Clear reporting is critical — a poorly written report 
  can delay incident response
- Reports must include timeline, indicators, and 
  recommended actions
- Communication between L1 and L2 analysts must be precise
